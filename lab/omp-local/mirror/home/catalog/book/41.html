RESEARCH METHODS:TIPS AND TRICKS FOR WRITING GOOD ARTICLES

Authors

Iswanto
Universitas Muhammadiyah Yogyakarta

Synopsis

This textbook is intended as a student's initial handbook in research such as making research reports and writing articles in reputable international journals. As a starting guide, of course, this book is far from sufficient to master research methodology. To master research methodology, this book teaches students the technique of using manager references. Therefore, literature or other sources are needed to complete this book.

Author Biography

Iswanto was born in Sleman, Yogyakarta, Indonesia, in 1981. He received his B.S, M.Eng and Dr degrees from Gadjah Mada University, Yogyakarta, Indonesia in 2007, 2009 and 2018. He became a Lecturer and Researcher in the Electrical Engineering Department at Yogyakarta Muhammadiyah University since 2010. His research is currently focused on Control, Robots, and Instrumentation. Scopus ID 56596730700. He is the editor in chief of the robotic and control journal (https://journal.umy.ac.id/index.php/jrc) which has been indexed by Scopus since 2021.
