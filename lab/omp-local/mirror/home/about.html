About the Press

ITK Press is under the command and responsibility of the Institute for Research and Community Service, Kalimantan Institute of Technology

Our main work revolves around planning, acquiring, designing, and distributing scientific knowledge to the public.

Vision

Supporting the vision of the Kalimantan Institute of Technology to become a superior university and play an active role in national development through the publication of journals, books, and other publications that contribute to the development of Indonesian society and the world.

Mission

  1. Increase the reach and influence of the Kalimantan Institute of Technology by publishing books, journals, and other publications that describe ITK's views and concerns for national development.
  2. Providing efficient and professional publishing and printing services for the Kalimantan Institute of Technology and the community.
  3. Improving the quality of human resources in order to realize superior printing and publishing.
  4. Develop networking to be able to synergize with other universities, industries, government, and society in the provision of education, research, and the development of innovation in a sustainable manner.
